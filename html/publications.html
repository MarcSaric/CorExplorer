Primary reference

The analysis on this website is based on the following publication.
Shirley Pepke and Greg Ver Steeg. Comprehensive discovery of subsample gene expression components by information explanation: therapeutic implications in cancer. BMC medical genomics, 10(1):12, 2017.

CorEx methodology papers

The papers below develop the theoretical foundations of CorEx.

  • The original paper introducing CorEx: Discovering Structure in High-Dimensional Data Through Correlation Explanation
  • A follow-up paper with generalizations used in the ovarian cancer paper: Maximally Informative Hierarchical Representations of High-Dimensional Data
  • A recent high-level review of CorEx methodology and applications: Unsupervised Learning via Total Correlation Explanation
